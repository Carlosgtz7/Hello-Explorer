Moon

The Moon is Earth’s most reliable teammate—the lockdown defender and veteran presence who doesn’t always show up on the stat sheet but changes the game. It sets hard screens by stabilizing Earth’s tilt, controls the boards by managing the tides, and keeps the tempo steady so the whole system doesn’t fall apart. The Moon may not score much, but it’s always in the right position, circling Earth like a trusted sixth man who knows their role perfectly. Without the Moon, Earth’s championship run would be way more chaotic—proof that even the greatest superstar needs the right supporting cast to win it all.
